My Childhood Story

My name is Khryxia Lheig M. Singson

When I was a child, my world was filled with laughter and joy, especially when I was with my cousins. We would spend hours playing games outside, creating our own little adventures in the backyard. Every weekend felt like a mini holiday as we laughed, played games, and made up our own adventures in the backyard. I remember feeling so happy and safe in our little world, surrounded by family. Even though I didn't have siblings at that time, my cousins felt like brothers and sisters to me. Those golden days will always hold a special place in my heart. Looking back, those childhood days were truly the best times of my life.
